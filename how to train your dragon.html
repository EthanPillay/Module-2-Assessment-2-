How to train your dragon 2

Actors: Jay Baruchel, Cate Blanchett, Gerard Butler

Age Restriction: PG-13

Showing Times: 12:00 PM, 3:00 PM, 6:00 PM

In How to Train Your Dragon 2, Hiccup and Toothless discover a secret ice cave full of wild dragons and a mysterious dragon rider. As a new enemy threatens their peaceful world, they must fight to protect their home and the bond between humans and dragons.
